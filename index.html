Frequently Asked Questions

I need support. What should I do?

Our dedicated support team is here to assist you 24/7. You can reach out via our live chat feature, email, or ticket. Additionally, we have an extensive knowledge base and documentation that can provide immediate answers to many common questions. Morever you can find support via discord community

What is the difference between VPS and Dedicated Server?

A VPS (Virtual Private Server) is a virtualized server that acts like a dedicated server but is hosted on a larger physical server. It shares resources with other VPSs. On the other hand, a Dedicated Server is a physical server dedicated solely to your needs, providing you with full control and access to all its resources without sharing.

Can I order additional IP addresses for my service?

Absolutely! If you need more IP addresses, simply contact our billing team or make a request through your client dashboard, and we’ll facilitate your requirement.

Can I increase the resources of my subscription?

Certainly! We understand that your needs might evolve over time. You can easily upgrade your hosting plan or add additional resources anytime through your client dashboard or by reaching out to our support.

Are there any bandwidth consumption limits?

All our hosting plans come with generous bandwidth allocations to ensure smooth operations. Specific bandwidth details can be found in your chosen plan, but rest assured, we’ve designed them to cater to high-traffic demands.

How can I access my Windows Server [RDP]?

You will find the login details in your mailbox. If you have Windows, you need to open “Remote Desktop Connection” on your computer. If you have macOS you should install “Remote Desktop” from the Apple Store, then in the “Computer” box, type the IP of your server. In the “Username” box, enter “Administrator”. Press “ok” and you will connect to the server by entering the password.

How do I get my access data?

Once your server is set up, the access data (IP address, username, password) will be sent to your registered email address. You can also find this information in your client dashboard.

What is your uptime guarantee?

We take pride in our robust infrastructure. We provide a 99.9% uptime guarantee thanks to the Tier 3 Data Center, ensuring that your website and applications remain available to your audience round the clock.

I would love to be your partner. How can I do that?

Where are your Servers Located?

Our primary data center are strategically located in Lithuania. 

Do you offer rewards for recommending you to my friends?

Yes, we value referrals! Through our referral program, you can earn rewards or credits for every friend you bring onboard. Visit our Referral Program page for more details.

What do you mean by "RDP"?

RDP stands for Remote Desktop Protocol. It’s a proprietary protocol developed by Microsoft that allows users to access a graphical interface of another computer remotely, often used for Windows servers. Generally RDP is also used as a way to refer to a Windows Server.

Are your servers DDoS Protected?

Absolutely! Our servers come with Anti DDoS protection for L4 and L7 Attacks to ensure your data remains safe and your operations uninterrupted, even during potential cyber-attacks. We also provide brouting defense against ssh, FTP and RDP attacks

Does your Servers come with root/Administrator access?

Indeed. All our servers, whether Linux or Windows-based, come with full root (Linux) or Administrator (Windows) access, giving you complete control over your environment.

How can I access my Linux Server?

For Linux servers, you can use SSH (Secure Shell) clients like Putty or Terminal. Enter your server’s IP address, port number, and when prompted, provide the root credentials.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?
